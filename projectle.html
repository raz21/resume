Medical academic network

LEARNefficient is a global medical academic network that enables LEARNers and Mentors to access and publish content in their topics of interest. They wanted a complete revamp of the web application to publish content in different areas of medical expertise. Through intuitive efforts and agile strategies, we crafted a user-friendly web application backend with Cake PHP and MySQL that integrates learners and mentors to access and publish content on their topics of interest.
