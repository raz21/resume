Pharmaceutical and Healthcare company website

Auxigen is a specialty pharmaceutical and healthcare company engaged in the research, development, manufacture, and marketing of high quality pharmaceutical and nutraceutical products.

It was a unique opportunity to work with a Auxigen as everything was built from the ground up, including branding and web design. I was ready to take on the challenges that come with a new company. Auxigen goals for their website included research, development, manufacture, digital marketing of high quality pharmaceutical and nutraceutical products, building traffic, and gaining popularity in the state of Indiana and Marion County.
