Healthcare App

Patient Buddy is an innovative Care coordination mobile health app that provides HIPAA compliant and secure patient communications, monitoring of adherence, and analysis of data to keep case managers and clinicians informed of the patient's status as well as providing a tool to educate the patient and caregiver and help them stay on track of their own care and the care of their loved ones.

It helps patients manage their health conditions by providing personalized support and information. It allows patients to track their symptoms, medications, and appointments, and provides them with educational resources and tips for managing their condition and supports healthcare providers and hospitals in automating and managing care pathways. The app also allows patients to connect with their healthcare providers and receive support from a community of other patients with similar conditions.
